About me

My name is Ian Miller, and I am currently a web developer based in New York City. I am a graduate of the Flatiron School, and I am primarily active in the Ruby and JavaScript languages, as well as the Rails framework. I was previously in the renewable energy sector, and I worked for the American Council on Renewable Energy, KMR Infrastructure, and Sol Vista in Washington, DC. I have also written a white paper on the Barriers to Tax Equity Syndication, which was published in May 2012. I am an avid traveler, and I try to take backpacking expeditions as often as I can. I lived in Australia for just under a year, and travel to San Francisco frequently.
